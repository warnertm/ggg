2024 Guys Gone Golfing

Chicago, Illinois

Embark on an unforgettable golfing journey through the heart of Chicago, where each course is a chapter in the city's rich golfing narrative. Begin your adventure at Sydney Marovitz, where the tranquil lakeside setting sets the tone for a day of pure enjoyment.

Continue your exploration at Belmont Golf Club, where the natural beauty and challenging layout promise an authentic golfing experience amidst lush greenery.

Next, discover the timeless elegance of Ravisloe Country Club, steeped in history and tradition, offering a round that's as enriching as it is rewarding.

Then, test your skills at Mistwood Golf Club, where the stunning vistas and strategic design provide a thrilling challenge for golfers of all levels.

Finally, conclude your journey at Harborside International, where the breathtaking views of the city skyline serve as a fitting backdrop to your final strokes. Welcome to the world of Chicago golf, where every tee time promises a new adventure and a deeper appreciation for the game.

Previous Trips

2023 Denver, Co

In 2023, we embarked on an epic road trip from Kansas City to Denver, dubbing it 'The Year of the Bus.' Our trusty 15-passenger bus became our home on wheels as we journeyed to the Mile High City. Some of us flew in, converging at our best AirBnb yet, setting the stage for a golfing adventure unlike any other.

This year, we focused on celebrating the artistry of golf course architecture, immersing ourselves in the history and design of each course we played.

Our first stop was The Golf Club at Bear Dance, where we were greeted by sweeping views of the Rocky Mountains. Designed by Jay Morrish and D.A. Weibring, Bear Dance is more than just a golf course – it's a testament to the land and its people. Each hole is meticulously crafted to blend seamlessly with the natural terrain, offering a challenging yet rewarding experience that pays homage to the indigenous heritage of the area.

Next, we teed off at Arrowhead Golf Club, a course renowned for its stunning red rock formations and breathtaking vistas. Designed by Robert Trent Jones Jr., Arrowhead is a masterpiece of modern golf architecture, seamlessly integrating the rugged beauty of Colorado's landscape into its design. Each hole is a work of art, inviting players to test their skills against nature's grandeur while uncovering the rich history that lies beneath the surface.

Our journey continued at Riverdale Dunes, a hidden gem nestled along the South Platte River. Designed by Pete and Perry Dye, Riverdale Dunes is a testament to the Dye family's legacy in golf course design. Its rolling fairways and challenging greens offer a true test of skill, while its history as a former military base adds a unique sense of nostalgia to the experience.

Finally, we found ourselves at CommonGround Golf Course, a public course with a rich history and a commitment to preserving the traditions of the game. Designed by Tom Doak and Renaissance Golf Design, CommonGround is a tribute to the golden age of golf architecture. Its minimalist design and strategic layout pay homage to the roots of the game, providing a sanctuary for golfers to escape the hustle and bustle of city life.

But our trip wasn't just about golf. We explored the vibrant city of Denver, indulging in local cuisine and culture. And of course, there were moments of laughter and camaraderie – like the time we accidentally almost walked off with someone's golf clubs! Yet, amidst all the excitement, the true highlight of our trip was the hours spent relaxing in the hot tub, sharing stories and making memories that will last a lifetime.

2022 Grand Rapids, Mi

This trip was designed after an espisode of Tourist Sauce by No Laying Up

Some highlights of this trip include our steak dinner at The Chophouse followed by cigars at BOLD cigar bar.

Our trip started at The Alpine Golf Course which is nestled in the rolling hills of Grand Rapids, Alpine Golf Club offers golfers a serene escape with its lush fairways and challenging elevation changes. Players can expect stunning views of the surrounding countryside as they navigate through strategically placed bunkers and undulating greens.

That afternoon we arrived at Pilgrims Run Golf Club which is known for its picturesque setting amidst dense forests and meandering streams, Pilgrim's Run provides golfers with a true nature retreat. The course features tree-lined fairways and natural hazards, offering a peaceful yet invigorating round for players of all skill levels.

After some R&R we started our next day of play at The Mines Golf Course which boast a unique history as a former gypsum mine, The Mines Golf Course boasts a distinctive layout characterized by rugged terrain and scenic beauty. Golfers can expect to encounter remnants of the mine throughout the course, adding an element of intrigue to their round.

Our final course was Diamond Sprng Golf Course which is a tranquil escape just outside of Grand Rapids, Diamond Springs Golf Course provides players with impeccably manicured fairways and scenic countryside views. The course's natural beauty, coupled with its challenging layout, ensures an enjoyable experience for golfers of all abilities.

2020 San Antonio, Tx

12 guys played 99 holes in 3 days

Picture this: a crew of avid golfers descending on San Antonio for the ultimate getaway. Some flew in, others took the scenic route, and a few hardcore souls drove through the night just to make that first tee time.

But Texas had a curveball for us - a blast of Kansas cold greeted our arrival. Yet, undeterred, we braved the 40-degree rain and hit the course with gusto. Our first stop? Breckenridge Park - a historic gem nestled in the heart of San Antonio. As we navigated its scenic fairways and challenging greens, we couldn't help but marvel at its storied past, dating back to the early 20th century.

Next up, Cedar Creek - a hidden oasis renowned for its lush landscapes and tranquil atmosphere. With its meandering creeks and strategic bunkers, this course tested both our skills and our senses, offering a true escape from the hustle and bustle of city life.

And then there was the Quarry. Oh, the Quarry 1 where golf meets adventure in the most unexpected of settings. Carved out of an old limestone quarry, this course is a marvel of modern design, featuring dramatic elevation changes and breathtaking vistas at every turn. It's no wonder it's consistently ranked among the top courses in Texas.

But our trip wasn't just about golf. We swapped clubs for cards, dove into lively poker games, and explored the vibrant Riverwalk scene. And who needs sleep when there's so much fun to be had?

Join us in reliving the grind, where every swing was a testament to our love for the game and every moment a cherished memory. Stay tuned for more adventures on our website - because in the world of golf, the journey is just as thrilling as the destination.
