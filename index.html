2024 Guys Gone Golfing

Chicago, Illinois

Embark on a golfing odyssey through the heart of Chicago's finest courses, where every fairway beckons with its own unique charm.

Begin your journey at Sydney Marovitz, where the shimmering lakeside vistas set the stage for a day of serenity and skill. Next, venture to Belmont Golf Club, where lush greenery and challenging holes await amidst nature's embrace.

Continue your adventure at Ravisloe Country Club, a timeless gem where history and elegance converge to create an unforgettable golfing experience. Then, test your mettle at Mistwood Glen Golf Club, where sweeping landscapes and strategic design promise a thrilling round for players of all levels.

Conclude your expedition at Harborside International, where panoramic views of the city skyline accompany each swing, offering a fitting finale to your Chicago golfing escapade. Welcome to a world where every tee time unveils a new chapter in the storied legacy of Chicago golf.
