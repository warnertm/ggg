2024 Guys Gone Golfing

Chicago, Illinois

Embark on an unforgettable golfing journey through the heart of Chicago, where each course is a chapter in the city's rich golfing narrative. Begin your adventure at Sydney Marovitz, where the tranquil lakeside setting sets the tone for a day of pure enjoyment.

Continue your exploration at Belmont Golf Club, where the natural beauty and challenging layout promise an authentic golfing experience amidst lush greenery.

Next, discover the timeless elegance of Ravisloe Country Club, steeped in history and tradition, offering a round that's as enriching as it is rewarding.

Then, test your skills at Mistwood Glen Golf Club, where the stunning vistas and strategic design provide a thrilling challenge for golfers of all levels.

Finally, conclude your journey at Harborside International, where the breathtaking views of the city skyline serve as a fitting backdrop to your final strokes. Welcome to the world of Chicago golf, where every tee time promises a new adventure and a deeper appreciation for the game.

Grand Rapids, Mi

This trip was designed after an espisode of Tourist Sauce by No Laying Up

Some highlights of this trip include our steak dinner at The Chophouse followed by cigars at BOLD cigar bar.

Our trip started at The Alpine Golf Course which is nestled in the rolling hills of Grand Rapids, Alpine Golf Club offers golfers a serene escape with its lush fairways and challenging elevation changes. Players can expect stunning views of the surrounding countryside as they navigate through strategically placed bunkers and undulating greens.

That afternoon we arrived at Pilgrims Run Golf Club which is known for its picturesque setting amidst dense forests and meandering streams, Pilgrim's Run provides golfers with a true nature retreat. The course features tree-lined fairways and natural hazards, offering a peaceful yet invigorating round for players of all skill levels.

After some R&R we started our next day of play at The Mines Golf Course which boast a unique history as a former gypsum mine, The Mines Golf Course boasts a distinctive layout characterized by rugged terrain and scenic beauty. Golfers can expect to encounter remnants of the mine throughout the course, adding an element of intrigue to their round.

Our final course was Diamond Sprng Golf Course which is a tranquil escape just outside of Grand Rapids, Diamond Springs Golf Course provides players with impeccably manicured fairways and scenic countryside views. The course's natural beauty, coupled with its challenging layout, ensures an enjoyable experience for golfers of all abilities.
