Sydney Marovitz

Sydney Marovitz Golf Course, located along Lake Michigan's beautiful shoreline in Chicago, offers a scenic and enjoyable golfing experience. This 9-hole course, nestled within the bustling Lakeview neighborhood, provides stunning views of the city skyline and the lake. Designed to challenge golfers of all levels, the course features well-maintained fairways and greens, along with strategically placed hazards. With its convenient location and affordable rates, Sydney Marovitz Golf Course is a popular choice for both locals and visitors seeking a memorable round of golf in the heart of Chicago. Additionally, the course offers amenities such as a clubhouse and practice facilities, enhancing the overall golfing experience.

Belmont Grove

Belmont Golf Course, nestled in the diverse Belmont-Cragin neighborhood of Chicago, offers an accessible and enjoyable golfing experience. Boasting an 18-hole course with lush fairways and challenging greens, it caters to players of all skill levels. The course's layout provides scenic views and strategic challenges, while its affordability and convenient location make it a popular choice for locals and visitors alike. Additionally, the facility offers amenities such as a driving range, pro shop, and clubhouse, ensuring a well-rounded golfing experience in the heart of Chicago.

Ravisloe

Ravisloe Golf Club is a historic and picturesque golf course located in Homewood, Illinois. It was originally designed by renowned golf architect Donald Ross in 1901 and has since undergone renovations to maintain its classic charm while offering modern amenities. The course features challenging holes with strategic bunkering and undulating greens, providing an enjoyable experience for golfers of all skill levels. Ravisloe's clubhouse offers excellent dining options and event spaces, making it a popular destination for golf outings, weddings, and other special occasions. With its rich history, beautiful scenery, and top-notch facilities, Ravisloe Golf Club is a must-visit for golf enthusiasts in the Chicagoland area.

Mistwood Golf Club

Mistwood Golf Club is a premier golf destination located in Romeoville, a suburb of Chicago. Renowned for its championship-level course design and exceptional facilities, Mistwood offers an unparalleled golfing experience. The course, designed by renowned architect Ray Hearn, features challenging yet playable holes set amidst picturesque landscapes, including water features and strategically placed bunkers. In addition to its top-tier course, Mistwood boasts a state-of-the-art practice facility, award-winning clubhouse, and upscale dining options. Whether you're a seasoned golfer or a novice, Mistwood Golf Club provides a memorable and enjoyable golf experience just a short drive from downtown Chicago.

Harborside Golf

Harborside Golf is a premier golf facility located on Chicago's scenic South Side. It features two championship courses: Port and Starboard, both designed by Dick Nugent. Offering stunning views of Lake Michigan and the city skyline, Harborside provides a challenging yet enjoyable golfing experience for players of all skill levels. The facility boasts top-notch amenities, including a spacious clubhouse, pro shop, and practice areas. With its convenient location and picturesque setting, Harborside Golf is a must-visit destination for golf enthusiasts in the Chicagoland area.
